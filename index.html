Glimpse Of Vastra

A brand that celebrates imagination, creativity and the very essence of sophisticated fashion, Vastra is not just any other brand you’d chance upon. It is an experience like never before. An experience that is sure to leave behind an optimistic residue for both the consumer and us. A brand that prodigiously appreciates your reflection and the personality that you’d like to be reciprocated on fabric, while carrying perfection and class to be our forte. Originality and authenticity go hand in hand with our process of dress making as you shan’t see anything like what our in-house artisans and karigars can weave for you! With a stern belief in quality, aesthetics and peculiarity, our major USP is that we deliver what we promise! No compromises, whatsoever. Come with us, to experience tasteful fashion with a revolution – Vastra – Dress To Your Moves.
