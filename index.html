Our Mission

Drones Will creates secure, reliable, and intuitive blockchain-based financial applications. We provide our customers with the ability to move money around the world quickly, securely, and for little to no cost. Our mission is to help create a world where all people and all businesses, regardless of socioeconomic status, location, or industry, can participate in and enjoy the benefits of a truly frictionless, global financial system. Our team has over two decades of combined experience in software design & development, financial technology & payment applications, and international business, finance, and global economics. Our combined experience has imbued each of us with a passion to build products that solve the big problems in our payment and financial systems; namely friction, cost, and exlusivity.
