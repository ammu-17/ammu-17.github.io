About Mercemur

Mercemur, with its latin roots which means 'We trade' is a platform to enable and help small businesses and creators create their own E-commerce website for themselves easily. Most Creators and Small Businesses like you start from selling what you have created with love in your social media or in your Friends & Family circles. We aim to simplify taking your business online in the digital world while you have your own real estate in the Internet. We Empower you to Grow and Shine as a community.

With Mercemur, you can launch your own business online under 15 seconds without worrying about the Development of the E-commerce Website, Maintaining it, Purchasing a domain etc., You will have your own custom domain and the Freedom to earn anything without paying Zero fees on commission to us. Mercemur is the smartest way to start your business in the Internet.
