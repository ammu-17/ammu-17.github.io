About Us

THE VASTRA

STORY

Being a woman who breathes passion and who wouldn’t want to be impeded by a pertaining, monotonous job, making something for others and touching their lives simultaneously was deep-rooted within me. It all started way back when I began up-cycling my own wardrobe, raided my mother’s and made pieces that were ethnically modern and had a great deal of taste and fashion. People kept asking me as to where I have been purchasing these dresses, while little did they know that I was the one making them. I have been a globetrotter all my life and getting to know about various people, cultures and the fashion factor that intertwined with it has always been my favourite hobby or more precisely my enthusiasm. Apart from having a sound knowledge about fashion and designs, I also am passionate about styling people with regards to their tastes and what compliments their features the most. For someone who constantly holds a pocket subconsciously to impact people in whatever I do, Vastra was more like my brain child through which I constantly am reminded of touching lives.

Vastra was born straight from my heart as a passionate pursuit towards converting timeless silk sarees into contemporary wearables. Wanting to create a clothing line that would carry forward the legacy of the silk sarees into the modern era along with giving a style statement to the one who’s wearing it was the notion behind the brand, initially.

We’ve been a proud conceiver of the concept and later thought why not as we extended our needles to designer wear, red carpet collections and catering to all wedding trousseau needs. What you see are pieces of originality and authenticity with a dash of class and a sprinkle of fashionable designing.

Across cultures and themes, Vastra has equipped and talented artisans who can weave magic with distinctive, inimitable design. It is just a matter of precision and detail while we sit at work with awe and inspiration in our eyes every time we touch our needles.

We have come a long way from where we originated and our exclusive new ranges stand testimony to that fact. Apart from our native collections and peculiar custom-made dresses, we cater to every need, every occasion and every style while still retaining the original flavour of ethnic contemporary. Vastra wholeheartedly strives at uniting the sole promise to celebrate beauty, the finest of fabrics and the very art of craftsmanship.

I welcome you abode to partake in my passionate journey called Vastra, as we celebrate beauty and life itself.
